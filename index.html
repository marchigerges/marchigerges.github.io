About

Somewhere between the pixels and the product decisions.

I came from social work and psychology before moving into UX and product design. That background changed how I think about products — I start with people, not screens.

I have worked with early-stage startups across food, safety tech, fitness, and fintech. Mostly sole designer, which means I have owned the full product thinking: what to build, why, and how to ship it.

Now moving into product management. I want to shape what gets built, not just how it looks.
