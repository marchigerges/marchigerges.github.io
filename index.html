About

Somewhere between the pixels and the product decisions.

I'm Marchi — a Sydney-based product designer moving toward product management. My background is in UX and product design, but the work I care most about is the thinking that happens before anything gets built: what problem are we solving, for who, and why does it matter?

I've worked with early-stage startups across food, safety tech, fitness, and fintech — mostly as the sole designer, which means I've had to be part designer, part strategist, and part product owner all at once.

I'm drawn to fast-moving teams where I can be close to the build while also having a seat at the table where decisions get made. That's the version of this work I want to grow into.
